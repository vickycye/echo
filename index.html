Our Product's Features

Through years of research and development, we have finally landed on a solution that caters to each individuals needs efficiently and effectively.

Voice-Activated Calling

Let's face it. Not everyone has access to a phone, which can be detrimental to the wellbeing of older individuals as they grow increasingly disconnected from our society. Our Voice-activated Calling technology enables the elderly to swiftly call any contact with minimal effort to seamlessly connect the older generation to modern technology.

Personalized Large-Language-Model

Having a personalized Large-Language-Model (LLM) allows for creativity and establishing a chat companion that dynamically morphs to the user's needs and routine. Not only does a Large-Language-Model improve the quality of life while using Echopod, but its ability to recognize behavioral changes is crucial to maintaining the wellbeing of the elderly.

Reminders for Medication

As people grow older, they tend to forget daily tasks and neglect their body, which is why we've developed Medication reminders to ensure that every user wakes up to a friendly nudge, which builds a strong habit over time. Even the most confusing medication schedules can be processed by connecting your Echopod account with your local pharmacy.
